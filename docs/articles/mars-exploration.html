Mars Exploration

Robotic missions and the search for life.

Mars exploration has included orbiters, landers, and rovers from multiple space agencies. Notable missions include Viking, Pathfinder, Spirit/Opportunity, Curiosity, Perseverance, and Ingenuity.

Science goals

  • Understand Mars’ climate and geology
  • Assess past habitability and search for biosignatures
  • Prepare for human exploration
